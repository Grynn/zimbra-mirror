About Zimbra ™ Collaboration Suite

The Zimbra Collaboration Suite is a full-featured messaging and collaboration application. ZCS offers  reliable, high-performance service along with advanced mail features, including advanced search capabilities, ability to sort mail by conversations, and tags to organize mail, shared and delegated calendar that supports subscribing to external calendars, web document authoring, tasks lists, as well as standard mail features such as address books, user-defined folders, and user-defined mail filtering.
