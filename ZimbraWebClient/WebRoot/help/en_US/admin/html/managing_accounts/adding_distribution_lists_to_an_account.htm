Adding Distribution Lists to an Account

You can add an account to a distribution list in either from the Account's Member of tab or the Distribution List's Members tab.

Adding from the account's Member Of tab

This is a quick way to add an account to multiple defined distribution lists.

  1. Select an account and click to Edit.

  2. Go to the Member Of tab and in Add distribution lists to the account area, click Search. All available distribution lists are displayed.  

  3. Select the distribution list(s) and click Add to add them to the Member Of list.

  4. Click Save and then Close.

Adding accounts to a distribution list from the distribution list

  1. Select the distribution list and click Edit.

  2. On the right, in the Add Members to this list area, either search for names from the company address book, or in Or enter addresses below section, type a complete mail address.  

To search for names in the company address book, enter a partial name or a complete name in the Find field and click Search, or leave the Find field blank and click Search. Names display, ten at a time, in the box below.

If the Find field is blank when you click Search, the search results return the all mail addresses in the address book. Use Back and Forward at the bottom of this box to scroll through the address list.

If you do not know the complete name, you can enter a partial name. Any addresses with that partial sequence in the name are displayed.  For example, if you searched for Sam, the results could include Samuel, Samantha, Sammy, Samson.

      • Note: Use Shift or Control to select more than one name at a time from the list.

  1. Click Save. The accounts are added to the distribution list and the accounts' Members Of tabs are updated to show the distribution list name.
