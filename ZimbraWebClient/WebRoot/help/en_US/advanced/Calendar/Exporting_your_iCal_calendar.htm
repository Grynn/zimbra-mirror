Exporting your iCal calendar

You can export your calendars and save the file in the ICalendar .ics format. iCalendars are the standard for calendar data exchange over the Internet, and .ics is the standard format for iCalendar information.  

Export is available from your Import/Export Preferences.

To export an iCal calendar:

  1. Select Preferences and click Import / Export.

  2. In the Export section, select the type as Calendar

  3. In the Source field, click to select the calendar to be exported.

  4. Click Export.

  5. Select where to save the .ics file and click OK.
