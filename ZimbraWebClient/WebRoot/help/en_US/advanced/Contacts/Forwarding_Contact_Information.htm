Forwarding contact information

You can forward contact in your address book as electronic business cards called vCards. A vCard is an electronic equivalent of a business card.

  • Select the contact name and right-click. Click Forward Contact.

  • Select multiple contact names in an address book and on the address book toolbar click Forward.

  • Within an email message, right-click the name in the From field and select Forward Contact.

A compose pane opens with the contact .vcf file attached. Complete the message and send.  The recipient can download the .vcf directly to their contact list.
