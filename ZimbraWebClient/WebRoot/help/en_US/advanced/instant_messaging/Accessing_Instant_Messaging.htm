Accessing Instant Messaging

You access Instant Messaging from the IM bar at the bottom of the ZWC Content pane. To start an instant messaging (IM) session, you click on the Buddy List and select a buddy to contact.

To verify that you appear online to others, confirm your status in the IM bar. If the status shows , you are ready to begin using IM. If it shows a different status, click the icon to open the  menu and change your status to online or available to chat.

You are now ready to start adding buddies.

Adding buddies

To use IM, you must have at least one buddy added to your buddy list. Buddies can be added from your Address Book or you can enter them manually.

To add a buddy:

  1. In the IM bar, click the . The Create New Buddy dialog box appears.

  2. Enter the email address of the contact you want to add as a buddy.

  3. Enter the Buddy Name as you want it to appear on your list. If you do not enter a buddy name, the contact’s email address appears as their name on the list.

The buddy is automatically notified and given the option to accept your add request.

  1. If you want to organize your buddies into groups, choose a group for your new buddy or select a group from the drop down arrow. The default group is Buddies. To assign a new group, type in the name of the new group and click OK.

 

  • To change your IM preferences and log in settings, see the Preferences > Instant Messaging folder.
