Changing status and availability

To use IM, your status has to be Online or Available to Chat so that your buddies can see you online and start a chat.

To set your status in Zimbra IM:

  1. Click the icon on the IM bar to open the status menu. You can set the any of the following statuses for yourself:

    • Offline

    • Online

    • Available for chat

    • Do not disturb

    • Away

    • Extended away

    • New status message

  1. Select the status that you want to show your IM buddies.

Alternatively, you can also click on the status display beside your name in the Overview pane on the left and choose a status from the drop down menu.

Creating a new status message

Zimbra IM gives you the choice of creating your own customized status messages.

To create a new status message:

  1. Click the icon on the IM bar to open the status menu.

  2. Select New Status Message.

  3. Enter the message that you want to set as your new status in the dialog box. Click OK. An example is “Will be right back”. Your customized status appears in the status menu.

To change your status to the default options available, click the Online drop down menu again and select your desired status.

  • Status messages you create are stored for future use and cannot be deleted. ZWC saves the five most recent status messages that you create for IM.
