Delete All Meetings in a Series

  1. Right-click on the appointment and select Series.

  2. Select Delete Series.

  3. Select either to delete all occurrences of the meeting from your calendar (both past dates and future dates) or to delete all references from this date and future dates. Click Yes.

  4. Click Edit Message to edit the email sent to attendees. Click Send to send the cancellation message.

The appointment is immediately removed from your calendar. An email is sent to the attendees, and the appointment is deleted from their calendars. You cannot undo a delete.
