Using Presence to Make Your Status Known

Note:  This feature is for ZCS Network Edition only and is available only in the Advanced Web Client.

(Cisco clients only)

Rich Presence information is displayed about you or a contact using the Cisco Jabber Client.

Presence status is displayed as available, away, on a call, or do not disturb. To see the presence status, hover over a contact card and the presence status of the contact displays. For example if you are on the phone, your presence status displays that you are on a call. You can also change your status manually from the presence drop-down menu.
