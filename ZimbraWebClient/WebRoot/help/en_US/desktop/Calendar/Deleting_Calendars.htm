Deleting calendars

You can delete any calendar in your Calendars list, except your default calendar.

  1. Right-click on the calendar to be deleted.

  2. Click Delete.

  3. On the Confirmation page, click Yes.

The calendar is immediately deleted. There is no undo.

Note: The meetings that were created from this calendar are not automatically deleted from invited attendee's calendars.
