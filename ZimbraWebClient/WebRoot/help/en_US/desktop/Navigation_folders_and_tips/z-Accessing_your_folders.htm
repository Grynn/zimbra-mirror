Accessing your folders

System folders for accounts you set up are automatically synchronized to the Desktop. If you setup a  Zimbra Web Client account, the content of all your folders are automatically synced between ZWC and your Zimbra Desktop application.

For your other accounts, the folders are displayed but empty.  To synchronize these folders to your account, select and right-click on the folder. Select Turn sync on. The folder will automatically be synchronized.

Note: This is a toggle. If you no longer want this folder to be synchronized, click Turn sync off.
