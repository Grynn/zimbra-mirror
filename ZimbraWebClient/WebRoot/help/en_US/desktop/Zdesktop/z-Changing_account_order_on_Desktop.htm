Organizing your Desktop View

When you set up Desktop, your accounts are displayed separately in the Overview pane. If you prefer to have all your messages in one account, enable the general preference Show the "All Mailboxes" section with my other accounts.  All messages from each of your accounts for Inbox, Sent, and Drafts are combined under this section. The number of unread items for all accounts is displayed. Your individual accounts are listed below All Mailboxes. You can click on an account to see only the items in that account.

 

 

When you view messages in the All Mailboxes view, an icon in the message list identifies which account the message is from.

 

Default Account

When you open Zimbra Desktop, your accounts are displayed in the Overview pane in the order that you set them up. The first account is the default desktop account. This is the account that is opened when you launch Zimbra Desktop. You can change which account is the default.

  1. Click Setup on the Zimbra Desktop. The My Accounts page displays a list of your accounts.

  2. To change the default account, click next to the account name. That  account is moved to the top.  

 

Besides being the account that is opened when Zimbra Desktop is displayed, the default account is the account used if Zimbra Desktop is configured as the default mail application. You can set this up from the Preferences>General page in the System Defaults section.
