Setting Desktop behavior for getting mail

In the Preferences>Mail>Displaying Messages section, enabling one of the options in When I click Send/Receive sets whether or not your view of the page you are on changes when you click Send/Receive.

    • If Run my default search is selected, when you click Send/Receive, the mail search is run for messages that should be delivered to your Inbox and your view is automatically redirected from the folder you were viewing to your Inbox view.  

    • If Update my current view is selected, when you click Send/Receive from any folder view, the request to get new mail searches for updates to your current folder view and for new mail in other folders. Your current folder view does not change.  If new mail is received to other folders, those folders are changed to bold and the number of unread messages is updated.
