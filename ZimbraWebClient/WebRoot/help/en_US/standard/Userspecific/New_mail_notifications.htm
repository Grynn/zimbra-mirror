Email Notification to Another Address

You can configure your mail options to be notified at another email address when you receive mail to your ZWC mailbox.  This feature may be useful if you do not receive a lot of mail to your ZWC account, but when you do receive mail, it's important enough to require a timely response.

To set notification:

  1. Click the Preferences tab.

  2. Click the Mail folder.

  3. In the Receiving Messages section, check Send a notification message to and enter the email address.

  4. Click Save. The notification feature is enabled immediately.
