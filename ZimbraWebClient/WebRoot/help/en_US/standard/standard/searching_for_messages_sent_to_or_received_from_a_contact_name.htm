Searching for Messages Sent to or Received from a Contact

You can search for all messages received from or sent to a specific contact name in your Address Book.

When you search within your Address Book, results from messages in all your folders in your account are displayed.

  1. Select a contact name from your Address Book.

  2. Right-click the message and select Find Emails.

  3. Select either Received From Contact or Sent To Contact. The search is executed and all messages in your account that meet your request display in the Content pane.
